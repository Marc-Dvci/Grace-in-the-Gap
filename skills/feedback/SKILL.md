# Rate a Grace moment

Require an 8-character Feedback ID from a displayed card and an integer rating from 1 to 5. Confirm the exact ID and rating, then call `grace_feedback` once. Explain that only approved content IDs and the numeric rating are stored locally; do not solicit or attach free-text spiritual feedback.
